MoRA:

Location representations powered by human mobility.

MoRA is a geospatial representation learning model that generates dense 128-dimensional vectors for locations. By anchoring satellite, demographics and POI data to a national-scale mobility graph, it captures the functional meaning of place for downstream intelligence.
